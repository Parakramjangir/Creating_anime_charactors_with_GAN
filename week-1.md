# Week 1: Introduction to Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs)

## Objective

The objective of this week was to understand the fundamentals of Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), their architecture, training process, and applications in image generation.

## What I Learned

* Basic concepts of Generative Adversarial Networks.
* Roles of the Generator and Discriminator.
* Adversarial training process and minimax optimization.
* Common GAN challenges such as mode collapse and unstable training.
* Overview of advanced GAN variants including DCGAN, WGAN, StyleGAN, CycleGAN, and Conditional GANs.

## Implementation

* Studied research papers and tutorials related to GANs.
* Explored GAN architecture and training workflow.
* Understood loss functions and optimization objectives.

## Results

* Developed a theoretical understanding of GANs.
* Learned how GANs generate realistic synthetic data through adversarial learning.

## Challenges

* Understanding the game-theoretic formulation of GAN training.
* Interpreting generator and discriminator loss behavior.
